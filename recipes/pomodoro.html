The Perfect Pomodoro Sauce

Description: The perfect tomato sauce for any pasta recipe

This recipe only uses five main ingredients - canned tomatoes, garlic, onion, basil and olive oil for the easiest, most delicious sauce ever

Ingredients

  • 28oz can of whole San Marzano Tomatoes
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • large white onion (yellow is ok too)
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1oz basil, stem on

Steps

  1. Finely dice onions and garlic; sweat onions and garlic in olive oil over medium heat
  2. Hand crush the tomatoes in a bowl; this will give the sauce a hearty, chunky texture (you can also puree in a blender or pass through a food mill)
  3. Raise heat to medium high and pour tomatoes and whole basil stems; once sauce is at a simmer, reduce heat to low and cook for 25 minutes
  4. Remove sauce from heat, take out the basil, season with salt and pepper, and add extra glug of olive oil for extra body
  5. Use the sauce immediately or store in a covered container for upto 1 week
